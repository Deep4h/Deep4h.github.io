Task and Challenges

Gameplay Programmer

I created the player with his abilities. The main challenge was to learn how to do a player because this project was my first project with Unity and C#, I only knew programming basics.

UI Programmer

I created a transiction Scene where the player can read the various pages of lore. The challenge here was to use Unity Componets to do details on the paper.
I've also created a Text System for visualize dialogues and lore

Audio Programmer

I've implemented and managed all the sounds and music. The challenge here was to play sound only when the text appear on the screen to realize a writing effect.

Sharau

Sharau is a 2.5D action-based Metroidvania with platform components for PC,playable with controller or mouse and keyboard.The player is going to play as Sharau, son of the God Ulgen, brother of Erlik. of Death. Elrik built a new Empire based on the fear that grew up in the wholeMongolia. Sharau, the only survivor of Ulgen ancestry, is now preparing himself forthe bloody revenge that Elrik deserves.

Babilon

Babilon si presenta come un Platform 2.5D orientato verso il trial and error che intende ispirarsi a titoli simili, rinomati del mercato Indie, quali “Celeste” e “Super Meat Boy”. Babilon è un classico esempio di platform che vuole offrire un’esperienza mirata principalmente legata al trial and error.
