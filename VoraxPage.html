Task and Challenges

Gameplay Development

Developing the gameplay for Vorax was an exciting and challenging process. I focused on creating an engaging, immersive experience by fine-tuning core mechanics, including resource gathering, combat, and exploration. Balancing difficulty was key, players needed to feel the tension of survival while being rewarded for strategic planning and quick thinking.

Gamescom: First Experience on the Other Side

Attending Gamescom for the first time as a developer was an eye-opening experience. Being on the other side of the event, away from just the crowds and booths, gave me a deeper understanding of what goes into showcasing a game at such a massive event. The preparation was intense, everything from the booth design and setting up demos to rehearsing presentations and ensuring everything ran smoothly. One of the biggest takeaways was seeing firsthand how important player feedback can be. Watching people interact with Vorax and listening to their reactions provided invaluable insights that we couldn’t get from just internal testing. The excitement of the audience, combined with the pressure of representing the game, pushed us to refine key aspects and focus on areas where players felt the most engaged or confused. Additionally, interacting with other developers and industry professionals opened up opportunities for collaboration and learning. It was inspiring to see how diverse the gaming industry is and how each developer approaches challenges in unique ways. The whole experience was an incredible mix of adrenaline, stress, and creativity that I’ll carry with me for future events.
